Swastik Bhardwaj

ACHIEVMENT

Youngest Classical Singer from India Book of Records

• Swastik has set a record for being the “Youngest Classical Singer” from India Book of Records in the year 2020-2021

Mega Finalist of DIL HAI HINDUSTANI (STAR PLUS CHANNEL)

• Swastik was mega finalist of International Reality Show” DIL HAI HINDUSTANI” which was telecasted on Star Plus Channel. He Competed contestants from all over world and made his position in Mega Round and performed in front of judges : Karan Johar, Shehkar Ravjiyani, Baadshah and Shalmali Kholgade

National Winner of Sangeet Natak Academy,Lucknow

Swastik has banged National Winner title consecutively from Sangeet Natak Academy, Lucknow in Khayal in the year 2017-2018 and in Thumri in the year 2018-2019 in Baal Varg Category

National Winner of Surtarang by Sangam Kala Group

Swastik was the National Winner of Surtarang which was organised by Sangam Kala Group in the year 2018

Scholarship Holder from CCRT (Centre for Cultural Resources and Training) in Hindustani Vocal Music

Swastik is Scholarship Holder from CCRT(Centre for Cultural Resources and Training), Ministry of Cultural in Hindustani Vocal Music in the year 2019-2020. He was just one candidate from all over Delhi which was choosen for this Scholarship.

Honoured from “Safalta Ke Sathi” Award for being an integral part of Hunar Haat Journey

Swastik was honoured from “ Safalta Ke Saathi” Award by Hunar Haat, Government of India . This was given to him for being an integral part of Hunar Haat Journey. He received this Award from Union Cabinet Minister Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi ji at Mumbai.

Delhi State Winner in Classical Vocal Music, by Directorate of Education

• Swastik has represented his school and was the Winner of Delhi State in Classical Vocal Music in junior Category. He has competed from more than 3,500+ school and banged First Prize.

Ghazal Sara Awardee from Rekhta Foundation 2021

Swastik has successfully recorded severals Sufi Songs and Ghazal from Rekhta Foundation and being Awarded from Ghazal Sara in the year of 2021

National Winner of Bal Kala Utsav in 2021

Swastik was the National Winner of Bal Kala Utsav in Hindustani Vocal Music in junior category in the year 2021

National Winner of Bal Pratibha Utsav by Parmaath Foundation

Swastik was the National Winner of Bal Pratibha Utsav in Hindustani Vocal Music and Semi Classical Vocal Music (Junior Category ) which was organized by Parmaath Foundation in the year of 2018.
